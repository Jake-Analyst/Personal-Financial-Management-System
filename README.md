The Personal Financial Management System is an intelligent, self‑contained dashboard that turns your M‑Pesa transaction statements into actionable financial insights. Simply upload a .csv, .xls, or .xlsm file and the system automatically analyses your income, expenses, savings, and spending patterns. It presents your data through interactive charts, real‑time KPI cards, and a dark/light mode interface. A built‑in AI assistant (powered by Google Gemini) answers your financial questions using your actual data, while the same AI engine generates smart, severity‑labelled recommendations and a one‑click PDF report. Designed to run entirely on your own computer or phone, it puts enterprise‑grade financial intelligence in your pocket
